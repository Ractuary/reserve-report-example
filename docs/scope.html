1 Purpose and Scope

Ractuary was requested by Self Insured, Inc. (SII) to provide an unpaid loss estimate for their workers’ compensation loss and allocated loss adjustment expense (ALAE) as of 2017-03-31 using data valued as of 2017-03-31 and additional information received through 2017-04-10.

The full scope of the analysis includes:

  1. Estimation of unpaid loss & ALAE as of 2017-03-31
  2. Presentation of results to management

This report is intended for use only by SII. Please advise RActuary of the release of this report to any other parties.
